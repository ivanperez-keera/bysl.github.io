Sketchnotes

I create large watercolor painted notes. I can effectively sketch topics in user experience and design, front-end engineering, and diversity plus inclusion. I've previously been hired to sketch AlterConf, Clarity, and Smashing Conf. I offer the notes to speakers as gifts at the conclusion of the event. Check out the archive of sketchnotes.

Open for sketchnote inquiries! Including conferences, events, and scoped branding projects. bysusanlin at gmail.


My terms:

  • A ticket to your event.
  • Travel (local transit and flights) covered. Plus hotel when applicable.
  • Market freelance rate paid by the day. Rates start at $2000 (USD).
  • If your event is not-for-profit and contributes to a good cause, we can work something out.

The works:

  • You may promote your event with an official sketchnoter.
  • I draw in realtime. Sketchnotes are shared while the conference is happening. I finish and share when each speaker wraps up.
  • I will cross-promote from my personal accounts before, during, and after the event.
  • You will receive high-resolution sketchnotes images and a PDF deck to share for present and future events.
  • Sketchnotes are offered to speakers at the end of the event as gifts.
