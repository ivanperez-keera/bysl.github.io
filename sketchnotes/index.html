Sketchnotes

Hire me to sketchnote your event. I can effectively sketchnote topics in User Experience, Design Process, and Front-end Engineering.

My terms:

  • A ticket to your event.
  • Travel and Hotel covered, if applicable.
  • Market freelance rate paid by the hour. Expect to cover hours preparing the set-up, time spent drawing talks, and post-processing.
  • If your event is not-for-profit and a cause I believe in, we can work something out.

The works:
  • You may promote your event with an official sketchnoter.
  • I draw in realtime, so you can live-tweet them during the conference.
  • You will receive high-resolution sketchnotes images to share for present and future events.
  • I will cross-promote from my personal accounts before, during, and after the event.
  • (Optional) Potential to sketchnotes to be done on thick, individual boards and gift them to your speakers! Speakers love being sketchnoted.

Sound good? Get in touch: bysusanlin.@gmail.com
