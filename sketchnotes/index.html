Sketchnotes

Hire me to sketchnote your event. I create big scale watercolor painted notes. I can effectively sketch topics in User Experience, Design Process, Front-end Engineering, and Diversity. I've previously been hired to sketch AlterConf, Clarity, and Smashing Conf. I also offer the notes to speakers as gifts. Check out the full archive of sketchnotes.

My terms:

  • A ticket to your event.
  • Travel (local transit and flights) covered. Plus hotel when applicable.
  • Market freelance rate paid by the hour. Expect to cover hours preparing the set-up, time spent drawing talks, and post-processing.
  • If your event is not-for-profit and a cause I believe in, we can work something out.

The works:

  • You may promote your event with an official sketchnoter.
  • I draw in realtime. Sketchnotes are shared while the conference is happening.
  • I will cross-promote from my personal accounts before, during, and after the event.
  • You will receive high-resolution sketchnotes images to share for present and future events.
  • Sketchnotes are offered to speakers at the end of the event as gifts.


Sound good? Please email bysusanlin at gmail.
