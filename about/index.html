About

Hello World; Susan is a Designer who codes at Treehouse and an Illustrator.

Previously, she worked on OpenTable's and Trulia's design teams where she built styleguides in addition to user experiences. She studied HCI at Carnegie Mellon. She believes the best technology designers are able to code and push their own pixels.

She sketches incessantly. You may have seen her sketchnotes out in the wild. She paints a Ghibli Stained Glass series and many watercolor trees. She believes an obsession of the craft exceeds all.
