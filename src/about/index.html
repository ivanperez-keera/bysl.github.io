About

Hello World; Susan is a Product Designer at Treehouse and an Artist, primarily painting in watercolor. She is currently traveling and working remotely around the world.

Previously, she built styleguides in addition to user experiences at OpenTable and Trulia. She studied HCI at Carnegie Mellon. She believes the best technology designers are able to code and push their own pixels.

She illustrates. You may have seen her sketchnotes. She paints many watercolor trees and is inspired by Studio Ghibli. She believes delibrate practice is the key to a successful art practice.
