UXD @ Trulia. I lead UX on consumer products from conception, flow, visuals, to implementation. Pictured, the Search Results Page embodying our latest, well received, experience language. I had a hand with the beta version of Suggestions. My personal growth mission has been to be better versed in HTML/Sass. I'm currently on the internal OOCSS library team. Designers venture into the world of Engineering. I spoke about the value of having a component library and the design process where it touches implementation, seeing your gorgeous mocks through the final 10% to shipping. I argued that designers need to undrestand their medium to best communicate with the awesome people building their designs. The end goal isn't to replace developers, but to speak the language.

This 15 minute deck was made for IxDA SF on a night of Design Doing
Previously, I've spoken privately for a visual design studio class and on a Carnegie Mellon BXA alumni panel

Once upon a time, Susan was an oil painter. Then she fell in love with technology and the people who built it during her time at Carnegie Mellon.
