Coding Designer & Illustrator

Hello World; I'm a person who likes to make things. I work at OpenTable on the Experience Design team. I graduated from Carnegie Mellon with a MHCI and BXA.

I draw incessantly. You may have seen my sketchnotes out in the wild. I paint the Ghibli Stained Glass series and Watercolor Forest. Hayao Miyazaki inspires me like no other.

I enjoy games and books. Ramona Flowers is my spirit animal. My hair has been blue, purple, pink, red, green, and blue. ❄
